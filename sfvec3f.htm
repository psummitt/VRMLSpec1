SFVec3f

Purpose: SFVec3f is a single-value field found in the DirectionalLight, LOD, OrthographicCamera, PerspectiveCamera, PointLight, Scale, SpotLight, Transform, Translation, and WWWInline Nodes. This field contains a three-dimensional vector. The SFVec3f field is written as three floating point values separated by whitespace.
