SFLong

Purpose: SFLong is a single-value field found in the PointSet and Switch Nodes. The SFLong field contains a single long (32-bit) integer. These fields are written as an integer in decimal, hexadecimal or octal format. Remember that hexadecimal format begins with 0x and octal begins with 0.
